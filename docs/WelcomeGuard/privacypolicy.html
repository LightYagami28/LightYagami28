WelcomeGuard - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 11/02/2025

At WelcomeGuard, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you interact with our Discord bot ("Bot").

1. Information We Collect

The Bot may collect the following types of information:

  • Discord User ID: To identify users within the server.
  • Server ID: To manage the interactions within your Discord server.
  • Captcha Responses: To verify users are human and prevent bot usage.
2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data solely for the following purposes:

  • User Verification: To ensure that only human users can interact with the server and prevent bots.
  • Server Management: To manage roles, greetings, and member interactions within the server.
  • Improving Service: To improve the Bot’s functionality and performance.
3. Data Storage

We do not store any personal information longer than necessary to perform the Bot’s functions. Data such as user IDs and CAPTCHA responses are temporary and are discarded after their intended use.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties. All data collected by the Bot is kept confidential and used exclusively for the functionality of the Bot.

5. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the information you share with us. However, please note that no method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
